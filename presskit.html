Description

Welcome to Meow Tower! Build your own tower for stray cats.

Solve Nonogram (a.k.a Picross) puzzles to buy furnitures and decorate your cats' room. Expand the tower and meet more stray cats. If you become friend with the resident cats, they might tell you a secret story!

Highlights

  • Cute and unique cats and variety of furnitures to collect.
  • Hundreds of simple but challenging Nonogram puzzles.
  • Reveal cats' hidden stories by completing their room.
  • Full support for offline play and cloud data backup.
